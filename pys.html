PAY YOURSELF FIRST

Paying yourself first is a simple concept, and easy to do. Whenever you start your budget, or get a paycheck, your first priority is to put money into savings, and "pay yourself first".

You should always save at least 10% of your paycheck, because it's a good balance between saving at least some money and also being able to pay what you need to afterwards.
